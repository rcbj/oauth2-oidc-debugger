Last Updated: July 14, 2026

IDPTools ("IDPTools", "we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting personal information.

By using the Service, you acknowledge the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Overview

IDPTools is designed as a client-side identity and access management (IAM) debugging and analysis toolkit.

As a core design principle:

  • Data entered into the tools is processed locally within your browser.
  • We do not intentionally collect, transmit, store, or process the identity-related information you enter into the tools.
  • We do not operate a backend service that receives user-submitted debugging data.
  • We do not maintain user accounts.

Because most processing occurs on your device, information you enter into the tools generally remains under your control.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Enter Into the Tools

The Service is designed so that information entered into IAM debugging tools remains within your browser.

Examples may include:

  • JWTs
  • OAuth tokens
  • OpenID Connect data
  • SAML assertions
  • Identity provider metadata
  • Authentication configuration data
  • Other debugging information

This information is not intentionally transmitted to our servers.

We do not intentionally store, retain, sell, share, or profile information entered into the tools.
